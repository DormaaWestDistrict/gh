DORMAA WEST DCE CUT SOD FOR THE CONSTRUCTION OF TWO FACILITIES.

The District Chief Executive for Dormaa West District, Hon francis Kwadwo Opong, yesterday, 6th March 2023, cut the sod for the construction of 1 no 6 unit classroom block at Presbyterian primary school , Nkrankwanta to enhance teaching and learning in the school. The contractor ' R. COSTAB assured the DCE that he would do his best to finish the project on time and hand it over to the assembly. The contract sum is about ghc 800,000, and the funding is from DACF. Speaking on behalf of the headteacher of the school, Mr. Samuel Owusu thanked the Npp government and the DCE for remembering the school as far as basic school infrastructure was concerned.

EMERGENCY WARD AT THE DISTRICT HOSPITAL.

Immediately after the sod cutting for the construction of the school, the DCE, together with his Assembly staff and Nananom of Nkrankwanta, proceeded to the Dormaa West District Hospital where he officially cut sod for construction of EMERGENCY AND ACCIDENT UNIT. The project, which funding, is DDF, is to be completed within a six months period by a contractor known as YAKANA VENTURES. At the hospital, the Chief of Nkrankwanta and Ankobea Dompemhene, Nana Ekyia Ekumbi II thanked the DCE and the Nana Addo government for various infrastructural projects in the Dormaa West District. He also entreated all stakeholders to act as supervisors to ensure the smooth running of the project. The DCE Hon. Francis Kwadwo Oppong assured the gathering of his commitment to ensuring that the Dormaa West District gets its share of the government's numerous projects. He was accompanied by party faithful led by Chairman Kwame Owusu.
